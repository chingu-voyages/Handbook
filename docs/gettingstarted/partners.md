# Our Partners

![Chingu and its Partners](./assets/Chingu_partners.jpg)

Chingu's mission is to help members turn what they've learned into the real-world experience needed to get a job. We don't provide training the way schools, bootcamps, or online courses do.

But sometimes members need extra training while they're in Chingu. To help, we've partnered with these organizations:

* [The Job Hackers AgileMBA](https://www.thejobhackers.org/participant/) — A program to help those in Agile roles (Product Owners and Scrum Masters) get the training they need to succeed.

* [Scrimba Pro Plan](https://v2.scrimba.com/?via=chingu) — High-quality technical training with a guaranteed minimum 20% discount for Chingu members.

We encourage you to check out these programs and take advantage of them.
